What is Ceramic Coating?

Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer applied to your vehicle’s exterior surfaces. Once cured, it forms a chemical bond with the paint, creating a hydrophobic (water-repelling) and scratch-resistant layer that offers long-term protection.

Is ceramic coating really worth it for my car or bike?

Yes, ceramic coating is absolutely worth it. It offers long-term protection against UV rays, dirt, scratches, and weather damage, while keeping your vehicle looking glossy and new. It's a smart investment for maintaining the value and appearance of your car or bike..

How long does ceramic coating last on a vehicle?

With proper maintenance, a professionally applied ceramic coating can last 2 to 5 years. At Hassonjee Automotives, we use premium-grade coatings designed for maximum durability and long-lasting performance.

Can ceramic coating be applied to older or used vehicles?

Absolutely! Ceramic coating is suitable for both new and used vehicles. We perform paint correction before coating to restore the shine and remove imperfections from older or lightly damaged paint surfaces.
