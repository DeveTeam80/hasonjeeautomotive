Got any Questions?
Do you service all Ampere electric vehicle models?

Yes. We provide expert servicing for all Ampere EV models including Magnus, Zeal, Reo, and more. Our trained EV technicians are familiar with the specific components and diagnostics of each model.

How long does it take to repair an Ampere EV?

Most common repairs like wiring faults, brake adjustments, or battery diagnostics are completed within a few hours. For major issues, we provide clear timelines during inspection.

Can you fix battery or charging problems in my Ampere EV?

Absolutely. We specialize in diagnosing and repairing lithium-ion battery issues, charging port problems, and controller faults using Ampere-compatible tools.

How often should I service my Ampere EV?

It’s recommended to service your Ampere EV every 3-4 months or after every 2000–3000 km, whichever comes first. Regular checkups extend battery life and ensure smooth operation.
