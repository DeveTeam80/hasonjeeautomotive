Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in “General Car Service,” and will you call me before extra work?
Our general service includes a full multi-point check fluids, filters, battery, brakes, suspension, tyres, AC, OBD scan, and a road test. If we spot anything outside the service scope (like worn brake pads or a leaking shock), we always call you with options and pricing before proceeding.
How long will a general service take? Can I wait at the workshop?
Most cars are completed in 2–3 hours, depending on the queue and any add-ons you pick (alignment, AC work, etc.). We do have a waiting area; many customers prefer to pre-book a slot so we can start right when you arrive.
Will using your workshop affect my manufacturer warranty?
No - routine maintenance following OEM guidelines does not void a warranty. We use genuine or OEM-equivalent consumables and record the work done. If your car is within free service period or has extended warranty conditions, tell us we’ll advise the best path.
Do you offer pickup & drop in Amravati?
