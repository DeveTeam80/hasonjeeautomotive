Monsoon Car Care Tips: Keep Your Vehicle Safe During the Rainy Season

The monsoon season in India brings relief from the sweltering summer heat, but with it come some serious challenges to your vehicle. From wet roads to flooded streets and poor visibility, the rains can affect the performance and safety of your car.


At Hassonjee Automotives, Amravati's most reliable auto service company, we understand how crucial it is to have your vehicle road-ready during this uncertain season. Here are some critical monsoon car care tips to make you drive fearlessly and remain safe on water-logged roads.

  1. Check Your Tyres – The First Line of Safety

    2. Your vehicle tyres are responsible for most of the grip on slippery and wet roads.

    1. Ensure your tyres are not less than 2.5 mm in tread depth
    2. Be aware of cracks, bulges, or uneven wear
    3. Keep the right air pressure neither too much nor too little

    Tip: If you drive often on heavy rain or muddy roads in and around Amravati, think about using rain-ready or all-weather tyres. Don't miss tyre rotation for even wear.


  2. Windshield Wipers - Don't Forget These Heroes

    3. Rain driving when visibility is poor can be hazardous. Your windshield wipers are your saviors during those times.

    1. Change worn-out or screeching wiper blades
    2. Inspect the rubber for cracks and resilience
    3. Refill your windshield washer fluid with a good anti-smear solution

    At Hassonjee Automotives, we provide speedy wiper changes and top-ups so you're never left stranded en route.


  3. Brake Check – Your Life Depends on It

    4. Brakes can behave differently in wet conditions, especially if water or mud interferes with the system.

    1. Test brake responsiveness regularly
    2. Listen for unusual squeaks, grinding, or vibrations
    3. Have the brake pads and discs inspected professionally

    We recommend a monsoon brake check-up at our service center to ensure your safety.


  4. Seal the Leaks – Protect the Interiors

    5. Nobody wants water seeping into the cabin or damaging electrical components. Here’s what to inspect:

    1. Door rubber linings and weather strips
    2. Windshield and window seals
    3. Sunroof channels (if available)
    4. Underbody rustproofing and corrosion protection

    Inquire with us about our monsoon protection package underbody rustproofing and complete seal inspection are included.


  5. Battery & Electricals – Moisture Can Be Tricky

    6. Electrical malfunctions happen more often in the rainy season. Ensure:

    1. Battery terminals are clean, dry, and securely connected
    2. All lights headlamps, brake lights, fog lamps, indicators are in good working condition
    3. Change dim or flickering bulbs as soon as possible

    Make the most of our Free Battery Health Check at Hassonjee Automotives this winter.


  6. Interior Care – Keep It Dry, Keep It Fresh

    7. Too much humidity can lead to unpleasant odors, fungus, and mold in your car. Here's how to avoid it:

    1. Use moisture absorbers or silica gel sachets
    2. Vacuum carpets and mats regularly
    3. Clean and sanitize AC vents and filters
    4. Use fabric protectants to prevent mold buildup

    Your car should smell fresh, not musty.


  7. Emergency Kit – A Monsoon Essential

    8. Don’t leave home without a basic emergency kit, especially if you’re heading out during heavy rains.

    1. Pack:
      Flashlight with extra batteries
      Jumper cables
      Tow rope
      First aid kit
      Spare tyre and necessary tools
      Umbrella or raincoat (because why not)

      car servicing in Amravati
