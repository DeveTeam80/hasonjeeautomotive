// FAQ
Got any Questions?
What’s the best protection option for my Toyota’s paint ceramic coating or PPF?

It depends on your driving habits and needs. PPF (Paint Protection Film) offers physical protection against stone chips, scratches, and debris ideal for highway or rugged use. Ceramic coating enhances gloss and offers UV, chemical, and water repellency. Many Toyota owners choose both for complete protection.

Do you offer special detailing packages for specific Toyota models?

Yes, we have curated detailing and protection packages for Toyota models like Fortuner, Innova Crysta, Camry, Urban Cruiser, and Corolla. Each package is designed considering the model’s usage, surface area, and finish type.

How soon after ceramic coating or PPF can I wash my Toyota?

We advise waiting at least 5 to 7 days to allow the coating or film to fully cure. Afterward, use a pH-neutral car shampoo and avoid automated car washes to ensure lasting protection and shine.

How long does ceramic coating last on a Toyota?

Our ceramic coatings can last between 2 to 5 years depending on the package chosen and maintenance practices. For daily-driven Toyotas like Innova or Fortuner, we recommend annual check-ups and proper wash techniques to maximize coating lifespan.
