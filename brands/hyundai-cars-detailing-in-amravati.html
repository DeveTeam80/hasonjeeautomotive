Which is better for my Hyundai ceramic coating or PPF?

It depends on your usage. Ceramic coating enhances shine and protects against UV rays, dirt, and light scratches. PPF (Paint Protection Film) offers physical protection from stone chips, scratches, and impact damage. For complete protection, many customers opt for both.

How long does ceramic coating last on a Hyundai vehicle?

High-quality ceramic coatings can last between 2 to 5 years, depending on the product used and how well the car is maintained. We offer both short-term and long-term coating packages.
