Depending on the package, ceramic coatings can last between 2 to 5 years. Our multi-layer ceramic coatings offer UV protection, high-gloss finish, and excellent resistance to contaminants ideal for Amravati‚Äôs sun, dust, and rain.

Can I wash my Audi right after getting ceramic coating or PPF?

We advise waiting at least 5-7 days before your first wash to allow proper curing of coatings or PPF. After that, use pH-neutral shampoo and avoid automated brushes for best results.

How often should I detail my Audi?

We recommend a complete detailing every 4-6 months. However, if you drive your Audi regularly in city traffic or dusty roads, more frequent detailing helps preserve the interior and paint finish.
