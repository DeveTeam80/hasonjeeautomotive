// FAQ
Got any Questions?
How long does ceramic coating last on a Mahindra vehicle?

Our professional-grade ceramic coatings typically last 2 to 5 years, depending on driving conditions and maintenance. We also offer maintenance packages to extend the life of your coating.

Is PPF better than ceramic coating for Mahindra SUVs?

Both serve different purposes! PPF (Paint Protection Film) is ideal for protecting high-impact zones from scratches and stone chips, while ceramic coating enhances gloss and makes cleaning easier. Many Mahindra owners choose to combine both for full protection.

Will detailing affect my car’s factory paint warranty?

Not at all. We use OEM-safe, paint-protection-approved products that are compatible with Mahindra’s original paint and finish. Your warranty remains intact.

Do you offer coatings for matte-finish Mahindra models like certain Thars?

Yes! We have matte-safe ceramic and PPF options that preserve the non-gloss finish while providing protection from environmental damage and stains.
