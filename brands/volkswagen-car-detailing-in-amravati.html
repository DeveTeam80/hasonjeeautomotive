// FAQ
Got any Questions?
What makes ceramic coating better than regular waxing?

Ceramic coating bonds with your car’s clear coat and offers months to years of protection, unlike wax that fades quickly. It provides hydrophobic shine, UV resistance, and minor scratch defense, keeping your Volkswagen looking new for longer.

Is PPF worth it for my Volkswagen?

Absolutely! Paint Protection Film (PPF) is perfect for high-impact areas like bumpers, ORVMs, and doors. It’s self-healing, protecting against stone chips, scratches, and scuffs ideal for Indian driving conditions

Will detailing remove all scratches from my car?

We use machine polishing and paint correction techniques that remove light to moderate swirl marks and scratches. For deeper damage, we’ll suggest touch-up or PPF as needed.

Which Volkswagen models do you cover?

We work on all Volkswagen models, including Polo, Virtus, Vento, Taigun, Tiguan, and more. Our team is trained in Volkswagen paint systems and interior trims, ensuring brand-specific care.
