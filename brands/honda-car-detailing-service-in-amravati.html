// FAQ
Got any Questions?
Is ceramic coating really effective for Honda cars?

Yes! Ceramic coating provides your Honda with long-lasting shine, UV resistance, and a hydrophobic layer that repels water, dirt, and contaminants. It’s ideal for models like the City, Elevate, and Civic, especially in Amravati’s harsh sun and dusty conditions.

What parts of my Honda should I cover with PPF?

We recommend PPF for high-impact zones like the front bumper, door edges, ORVMs, and trunk lip. For maximum protection, some owners choose full-body PPF especially on models like the Elevate or WR-V.

How often should I get my Honda detailed?

Ideally, every 4 to 6 months. Regular detailing preserves the paint, prevents interior wear, and keeps your Honda looking and smelling fresh perfect for daily commuters like the Amaze or family cars like the Jazz.

Will detailing remove tough stains and odors from my car's interior?

Yes! Our steam cleaning and fabric shampooing break down deep stains, while anti-bacterial disinfection removes odors, germs, and allergens restoring your Honda’s cabin to a like-new condition.
