Is PPF better than ceramic coating for Mercedes-Benz?

Both offer unique benefits. PPF (Paint Protection Film) is best for physical protection against stone chips, scratches, and swirl marks. Ceramic coating offers gloss, water repellency, and ease of cleaning. Many Mercedes-Benz owners choose both for comprehensive protection.

Can I apply ceramic coating on top of PPF?

Yes, absolutely! In fact, we recommend it. Applying ceramic coating over PPF adds extra hydrophobic properties and shine while making maintenance easier. It‚Äôs the ultimate combination for long-lasting protection and aesthetics.

How soon can I wash my Mercedes after ceramic coating or PPF?

Wait at least 5-7 days after the application to allow proper curing. Post that, wash your Mercedes using pH-neutral car shampoo and avoid automatic car washes with hard bristles.
