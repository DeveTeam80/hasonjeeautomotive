The Ultimate Car General Service in Amravati: 20-Point Checklist at Hassonjee

When you drop your car off at a workshop, do you know exactly what you are paying for? Many drivers in Amravati worry about hidden costs or incomplete work. Driving on roads like Badnera Road or through the city traffic puts immense stress on your engine and suspension.

At Hassonjee Automotives, we believe in 100% transparency. Whether you drive a Maruti Swift or a luxury BMW, our periodic maintenance service covers 20 critical points to ensure your vehicle is safe and efficient. Here is exactly what is included in car service at our workshop.


1. Essential Fluids & Filter Check

Fluids are the lifeblood of your car. In the heat of Amravati, fluids degrade faster. Our service includes:

  • Engine Oil Replacement: We use high-grade synthetic or mineral oil suitable for your specific engine.
  • Coolant Top-up: Preventing overheating during summer traffic.
  • Brake Fluid Check: Ensuring your brakes respond instantly.
  • Air Filter Cleaning/Replacement: Crucial for mileage, especially in dusty conditions.
  • AC Filter Cleaning: To keep your cabin cooling efficient.

2. Safety & Brakes Inspection

Your safety is our priority. We perform a rigorous inspection of the braking system, which is often the most neglected part of car general service in Amravati.

  • Brake Pad Thickness: Checking for wear and tear.
  • Brake Disc/Drum Scoring: Inspecting for deep scratches that cause vibration.
  • Handbrake Adjustment: Ensuring the car holds slope.
  • Tyre Tread Depth: Checking if your MRF or other tyres need replacement.

3. Electrical & Vision Check

Modern cars rely heavily on electronics. A weak battery can leave you stranded.

  • Battery Health Check: We test the voltage of your Exide or Amaron battery.
  • All Lights Inspection: Headlights, indicators, brake lights, and cabin lights.
  • Wiper Blade Check: Ensuring clear vision for the monsoon.
  • Instrument Cluster: Scanning for any warning lights (Check Engine, ABS, Airbag).

4. Why Choose Hassonjee as Your Car Service Center in Amravati?

Unlike unauthorized local garages, we use Manufacturer Recommended tools and scanners. We don't just "wash and wipe." We diagnose. Plus, being a multi-brand workshop, we offer dealership-level quality at a much more affordable price point.
