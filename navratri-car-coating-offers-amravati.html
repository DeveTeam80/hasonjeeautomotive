Offer 1: Ceramic Coating - ₹15K + 3 Years Warranty

Ceramic coating is the gold standard for everyday shine and protection in Indian driving conditions. It bonds with the factory clearcoat to form a durable, hydrophobic layer that resists dust, UV, bird droppings, and road grime perfect for Amravati’s mixed urban-highway use.

  • Deep, wet gloss: Rich, mirror-like reflections that make colors pop.
  • Hydrophobic water beading: Rain and wash water sheet off faster, reducing water spots.
  • UV and chemical resistance: Helps slow paint oxidation and etching.
  • Easier cleaning: Dirt and tar stick less, so wash time drops.

Navratri Special Includes: Paint inspection → foam & contact wash → iron/tar decontamination → clay treatment → single-/dual-stage paint correction (as needed) → ceramic coat application → curing → final QC. You also get post-care guidance to keep that gloss festival-fresh.


Offer 2: Graphene Coating - ₹19K + 4 Years Warranty

Graphene coatings are the new benchmark for slickness and heat management. They’re engineered to boost surface smoothness and add robust chemical resistance ideal if you park outdoors or drive long distances.

  • Ultra-slick finish: Dust and contaminants release even more easily.
  • Excellent water behavior: Tight, uniform beads; faster sheeting.
  • Heat & chemical resilience: Helps the surface stay cooler and resist harsh fallout.
  • Enhanced swirl resistance: A smoother surface that stays glossier longer.

Navratri Special Includes: The same meticulous prep workflow as ceramic because great results start with great prep followed by a professional graphene application, curing, and final inspection.

  • 3-Year Warranty: Peace of mind with documented job details.

Who Benefits Most?

  • New car owners: Lock in the showroom gloss from day one.
  • Daily drivers: Resist swirls, grime, and UV - spend less time washing.
  • Highway commuters: Easier bug/tar removal; better long-term clarity.
  • Dark-color cars: Noticeably deeper shine and fewer visible swirls.

Simple After-Care Tips

  • Use a pH-neutral shampoo; avoid harsh detergents.
  • Wash with two-bucket method and plush microfiber.
  • Dry with a clean microfiber drying towel (no circular rubbing).
  • Quick detailer sprays (coating-safe) help maintain slickness between washes.
  • Avoid automatic brushes; opt for touchless or hand washes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Ceramic vs Graphene - what should I choose?
Both protect and shine. Ceramic is the proven all-rounder for deep gloss and hydrophobics. Graphene adds a touch more slickness, stain/heat resistance, and ease of cleaning. Pick ceramic for classic gloss or graphene for the newest tech feel.
Will coating remove scratches and dents?
No. Coatings do not fill deep scratches or fix dents. Our package includes paint correction to reduce light swirls/haze before coating. Deep scratches, chips, or dents require separate bodywork/paint correction services.
How long does the coating actually last?
Your Navratri offer includes a 3-year warranty, contingent on proper wash care. With gentle washing and periodic check-ups, expect long-term gloss and protection throughout the warranty term.
How much time do you need and do I have to leave the car?
Plan for 1 full day for most cars (prep + application + curing). Depending on paint condition and workload, we may advise keeping the car overnight to ensure optimal curing and quality checks.
