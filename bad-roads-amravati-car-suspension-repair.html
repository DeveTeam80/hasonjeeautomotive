Bad Roads in Amravati? 5 Signs Your Car Suspension Needs Immediate Attention

We all love our city, but let's be honest driving in Amravati can be a bumpy experience. From the uneven patches on Badnera Road to the potholes that appear every monsoon near MIDC, your car takes a beating every day. While your tyres absorb some of the shock, it's your car's suspension system that does the heavy lifting.

A damaged suspension isn't just about comfort; it's a safety hazard. At Hassonjee Automotives, we often see customers ignore small signs until they turn into expensive repairs. Here are 5 tell-tale signs that your car needs a suspension check-up immediately.


1. Excessive Bouncing on Amravati Roads? Check Your Suspension

The most obvious sign is a rough ride. If you can feel every single pebble on the road, or if your car bounces excessively after hitting a bump, your shock absorbers or struts are likely worn out. Your car should settle quickly after a bump; if it continues to bounce, visit us at Hassonjee Automotives for a check-up.


2. Car Pulling Left? You Need 3D Wheel Alignment in Amravati

Have you noticed that you're constantly fighting the steering wheel to keep the car straight? If your car drifts to the left or right when you're driving on a straight road like the Amravati-Nagpur highway, this is a classic sign of bad Wheel Alignment. This often happens after hitting a deep pothole.


3. Nose Dives & Longer Braking: Signs of Worn Shock Absorbers

This is dangerous. If the front of your car dips forward (nose-dives) when you apply the brakes firmly, your front shocks are shot. This can increase your stopping distance by up to 20%, which can be the difference between a safe stop and an accident in Amravati city traffic.


4. Premature Tyre Wear: Save Cost on MRF Tyres

Take a look at your tyres. Is the tread wearing down faster on the inside or outside edge compared to the center? This uneven wear is a direct result of suspension issues or poor alignment. Ignoring this means you'll have to replace your expensive tyres much sooner than necessary.


5. Oil Leaks & Noises: Expert Car Repairing Service in Amravati

If you look under the wheel arch and see oil leaking from your shock absorbers, they need replacement. Also, listen for squeaking, creaking, or clunking sounds when driving over speed breakers or turning corners. These are the sounds of your suspension crying for help.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my car suspension is damaged due to bad roads?
The most common signs include a bumpy or rough ride, the car pulling to one side (alignment issue), uneven tyre wear, and strange clunking noises when going over speed breakers.
Is it safe to drive with a broken suspension in Amravati?
No, it is not safe. A damaged suspension affects your braking distance and steering control, which can be dangerous on busy Amravati roads. It also leads to faster tyre wear.
Does Hassonjee Automotives offer Wheel Alignment and Balancing?
Yes, we do! We use advanced 3D Wheel Alignment technology to ensure your car drives straight and your tyres last longer. We recommend checking alignment every 5,000 km or after hitting a major pothole.
