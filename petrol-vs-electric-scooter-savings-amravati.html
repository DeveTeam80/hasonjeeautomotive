Petrol vs. Electric Scooter: How Much Can You Save in Amravati Monthly?

With petrol prices in Amravati consistently hovering over ₹105 per litre, your daily commute from Rajapeth to Badnera or city errands is becoming an expensive affair. Many residents are asking: Is it really worth switching to an EV?

As the leading Ampere electric scooter dealer in Amravati, Hassonjee Automotives has crunched the numbers for you. We’ve built a realistic petrol vs EV cost calculator based on local electricity rates and road conditions. The savings are massive.


1. The Cost Breakdown: Petrol vs. EV

Let's assume a daily commute of 30km (common for Amravati office goers and students). Here is the monthly math:

Parameter Petrol Scooter (110cc) Ampere Electric Scooter
Mileage / Range 45 km/litre 85-100 km/charge
Fuel Price ₹ 106.00 / litre ₹ 10.00 / unit (Electricity)
Running Cost per KM ~ ₹ 2.35 ~ ₹ 0.15 - ₹ 0.20
Monthly Fuel Cost (900km) ₹ 2,115 ₹ 180

The Verdict: You save approximately ₹ 2,000 every single month just on fuel. That is ₹ 24,000 in savings annually!


2. Maintenance Savings: Zero Engine Troubles

The savings don't stop at the petrol pump. A standard petrol scooter has hundreds of moving parts—pistons, spark plugs, oil filters, and belts.

An Ampere Electric Scooter essentially has a battery and a motor.

  • No Oil Changes: Forget about quarterly oil servicing costs.
  • No Filter Replacements: No air filters or oil filters to buy.
  • Low Wear & Tear: Regenerative braking saves your brake pads.

3. Electric Bike Price in Amravati

Many customers worry about the upfront cost. However, when you factor in the fuel savings, the scooter effectively pays for itself in roughly 2.5 to 3 years.
