Summer BMW Maintenance in Amravati

Amravati’s summers are harsh and dry, with high UV exposure and extreme cabin heat. Protect your BMW with these summer care tips:

Ceramic Coating for Paint Protection

Get a professional ceramic coating in Amravati at Hassonjee Automotives. It shields your BMW’s paint from UV damage, oxidation, and fading.

Ensure your AC system is serviced before peak summer. Cabin filters must be clean for fresh airflow.

High heat drains BMW batteries faster. Our technicians inspect coolant, brake fluid, and engine oil to prevent overheating.


Monsoon BMW Maintenance in Amravati

Monsoons bring mud, waterlogging, and high humidity—challenging for any luxury car. Here’s how to keep your BMW safe:

Underbody Anti-Rust Treatment

At Hassonjee Automotives, we apply underbody protection to prevent rust from Amravati’s rain-soaked roads.

Windshield & Wiper Care

Crystal-clear visibility is a must. We recommend professional glass treatment for water repellency and wiper blade replacement.

Interior Detailing for Moisture Control

Our BMW interior detailing in Amravati includes deep cleaning and leather conditioning, protecting against mold, dampness, and odor.


Winter & Dusty Season BMW Maintenance

Amravati experiences a dry, dusty winter season. Dust can harm BMW’s engine, paintwork, and interiors.

Engine Bay Cleaning

Dust accumulates quickly under the hood. Our expert detailing team ensures a safe, waterless engine bay clean-up.

Air Filter & Cabin Filter Check

Frequent filter checks maintain performance and ensure clean air circulation inside your BMW.

Regular Washing & Waxing

Even in winter, dust particles can cause micro-scratches. Routine washes with premium wax or ceramic coating add extra protection.
